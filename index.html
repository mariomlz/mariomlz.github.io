Bio

Hello! I am Mario Malizia, a Research Engineer in Robotics at the Robotics and Autonomous System Unit of the Royal Military Academy of Belgium, supervised by Geert De Cubber and Rob Haelterman. I focus on field robotics, specifically on improving human operator perception during mine removal.
