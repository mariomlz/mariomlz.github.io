Bio

Hello! I am Mario Malizia and I am a Research Engineer in Robotics at the Robotics and Autonomous System Unit of the Royal Military Academy of Belgium where I am supervised by Geert De Cubber and Rob Haelterman.

I am focusing on field robotics, specifically on how to improve human operator perception in the phase of mine removal.
